About

I am a researcher at ETRI working on machine learning, computer vision, and biosignal processing. My current focus is IR-based remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) for pulse estimation from wearable devices such as smart glasses. I am broadly interested in robust signal recovery under real-world illumination, synthetic data augmentation, and attention-based models for physiological sensing.
